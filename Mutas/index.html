Mutas - A Modular Tabletop Role-Playing Game

Modular in that I am designing it so that players can make it complex or simple in the ways that are interesting to them. Admittedly, this does mean designing the some of the sub-systems multiple times, but that is part of the charm. In a way it also means that I don't have decide what the correct way is because players can choose their own version of correctness.
